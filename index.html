⬅️ Namnet på ditt unsigned preset i Cloudinary
Skapa upload preset:
1. Gå till Cloudinary → Settings → Upload → Upload presets
2. Klicka "Add upload preset"
3. Namn: ncc (minuskler)
4. Signing mode: Unsigned
5. Asset folder: underlag
6. Klicka "Save"
